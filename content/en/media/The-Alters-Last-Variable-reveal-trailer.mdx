<Section icon={Shield} title="Underground Base and Cryosleep Survival Rules">

Unlike the mobile base of the main game, your underground facility cannot outrun environmental hazards. Survival is a game of resource management, split between short cool phases and long, 13-year hot phases where the crew must enter cryosleep.

| Survival Objective | Timing / Deadline | Resource Requirements | Failure Consequence |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Repair Research Lab** | Cycle 1, Day 1 | 1 Repair Kit (10 Metals) | Blocks Alter creation and technology tree |
| **Build Cryosleep Chamber** | Cycle 1, Day 14 | 50 Metals, 65 Rapidium | Instant game over; crew cannot survive hot phase |
| **Establish Food Pipeline** | Cycle 1, Day 5 | Greenhouse, Organics Outpost | Morale collapse, starvation, and Alter revolts |
| **Reserve Cryosleep Organics** | Cycle End (Pre-Sleep) | 50 Organics per person in base | Clones cannot enter stasis; permanent death |

<Callout type="warning" title="The Cryosleep Checklist">
Before triggering cryosleep at the end of a cycle, ensure you have exactly 50 Organics stored for each person remaining inside the base. Dismantle surface structures to recoup materials, heal all injuries, and complete outstanding Alter conversations.
</Callout>

</Section>
