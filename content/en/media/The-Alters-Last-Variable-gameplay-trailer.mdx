<Section icon={Shield} title="Underground Base Layout & Cryosleep Cycles">

The underground facility must be expanded carefully to accommodate new machinery and dormitory space. Because different soil types limit where you can build, planning your layout early prevents expensive reconstruction projects.

Each cool phase serves as a preparation window before a massive hot phase forces your crew back into cryosleep. You must secure a strict reserve of Organics before the final day of the cycle.

<Callout type="warning" title="Organics Reservation Warning">
Each person entering the Cryosleep Chamber requires 50 Organics. If you have Jan Scientist and two active Alters, you must keep 150 Organics untouched in your inventory. Failing this requirement prevents you from ending the cycle safely.
</Callout>
