<Section icon={Shield} title="Underground Base and Cryosleep Management">

The underground base in Last Variable introduces new constraints, specifically regarding soil types and module placement. Unlike the mobile base of the original game, this facility is permanent, requiring careful long-term planning for corridors and power lines. The most critical addition is the **Cryosleep Chamber**, which must be constructed before the end of the first 14-day cycle.

| Resource | Required for Chamber | Source Location | Priority |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Metals** | 50 Units | Southern Metal Outpost | **High** |
| **Rapidium** | 65 Units | Oasis Perimeter Deposits | **High** |
| **Organics** | 50 per Person | Greenhouse / Surface Scavenging | **Critical** |
| **Phased Rapidium**| Research Only | Laboratory Processing | Medium |

Survival depends on your ability to prepare for the "Hot Phase," a years-long period where the surface is uninhabitable. During this time, the crew enters cryosleep, consuming a fixed amount of Organics.

<Section icon={Target} title="Terraforming Arenas and Resource Routes">

The expansion map consists of nine interconnected arenas, each requiring a specific Tier of Terraformer to unlock its full potential. Terraforming not only changes the environment but also exposes new resources and "Old Technology Debris" required for advanced experiments.

| Arena Name | Terraformer Tier | Key Resources | Unique Feature |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Oasis** | N/A (Reference) | Electric Algae | The central story hub |
| **Rocky Valley** | Tier I | Metals / Obsidian | First Field Lab location |
| **Misty Gorge** | Tier II | Enriched Metal | High verticality / Roots |
| **Far Hill** | Tier III | Megafluid Inputs | Final research site |

The progression through these arenas is gated by your research into root-barrier drilling and radiation protection. As you terraform more regions, the planet's "defensive reactions"—such as increased seismic activity—will become more frequent. This creates a risk-reward dynamic where faster expansion provides more resources but puts more strain on your base's repair systems.

<Callout type="info" title="Research Tip">

</Callout>
