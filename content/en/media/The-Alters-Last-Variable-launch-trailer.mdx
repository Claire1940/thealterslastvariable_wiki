<Section icon={Shield} title="Getting Started: How to Access and Survival Basics">

Starting the new campaign is straightforward, but players should be aware that it functions as a separate progression route. Your base-game structures and inventory do not carry over. The expansion introduces a high-stakes environment where the underground base serves as your only refuge against the planet's hostile atmosphere.

**Video Highlights:**
- The trailer showcases the transition from the surface to the new underground research core.
- Visuals reveal the four specialist Alters working in tandem within the Laboratory.
- Scenes highlight the massive scale of the Tier III Terraformers in the Waterfall region.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Installation and Verification">
    Update *The Alters* to the latest version. Confirm the *Last Variable* DLC is listed as "Installed" in your platform's library management settings.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Menu Navigation">
    Launch the game and select **Start New Game**. Choose the **Last Variable** campaign from the menu. You do not need a completed save file to begin.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Initial Repairs">
    Complete the "Famous Last Words" quest. Use your starting Metals to build a Repair Kit and restore the damaged Research Lab immediately.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="The 14-Day Deadline">
    Track the **Forced Dormancy** objective. You must construct the Cryosleep Chamber within 14 days to survive the first planetary cycle.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Resource Management">
Do not spend your initial Metal reserve on comfort modules. Prioritize the **Research Lab** and **Metals Outpost** to ensure you can meet the Cryosleep Chamber construction costs.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Trophy} title="Endings, Choices, and Achievements">

The narrative of *Last Variable* concludes with a profound ethical dilemma regarding the Oasis and the cost of scientific obsession. Your interactions with **JanBot** and the fate of your specialist Alters will determine which of the two primary endings you witness.

**Major Ending Paths:**
- **Create a Successor**: Jan accepts the necessity of an endless research cycle, creating a new Alter to carry on the work indefinitely.
- **Break the Cycle**: Jan rejects the succession model, choosing to end the project and accept mortality rather than creating more lives for a singular purpose.
