<Section icon={Shield} title="Underground Base Management and Cryosleep Cycles">

The underground base serves as your sanctuary. Unlike the surface, which is subject to extreme temperature shifts, the base allows the crew to survive the 13-year "hot phases" through cryopreservation.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Restore the Research Core">
    Immediately build a Repair Kit (10 Metals) to fix the Research Lab. Without this, you cannot access the Alter branching menu or terraforming technology.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The 14-Day Deadline">
    You must construct the **Cryosleep Chamber** (50 Metals, 65 Rapidium) before the end of the first 14-day cool phase. Failure to do so results in an immediate game over.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Field Lab Deployment">
    Before entering cryosleep, you can assign one Alter to a surface Field Lab. This Alter will continue researching for 13 years while the rest of the crew sleeps, but they will visibly age and be unavailable for base work upon waking.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Resource Buffering">
    Every Jan entering cryosleep requires **50 Organics**. Ensure your Greenhouse or Organics Outposts are producing enough to cover the entire crew before the cycle ends.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Soil Restrictions">
The underground base is limited by soil types. Some cells are unbuildable, forcing you to plan your module layout carefully. Keep sleeping quarters away from noisy production modules to maintain crew morale.
</Callout>

</Section>

| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Phased Rapidium**| Rapidium + Metal | Workshop | Tier I & II Terraformers |

### Research Build Order for Cycle 1
1. **New Branching Procedure**: Unlocks the ability to create your first Alter.
2. **Analysis of Samples**: Required to understand the Oasis ecosystem.
3. **Phased Rapidium**: Necessary for building the first Terraformer in Rocky Valley.
4. **Terraformer Technology**: The primary tool for map expansion.

<Callout type="tip" title="Efficiency Hack">
Assign **Jan Physicist** to the Workshop when crafting Phased Rapidium. He reduces the crafting time by 50%, allowing you to stockpile materials before the cryosleep deadline.
</Callout>
