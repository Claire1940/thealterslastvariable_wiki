Before you can reach the finale, you must navigate 14-day cryosleep cycles and manage a scientific crew consisting of four specialist Alters. Each decision you make regarding resource allocation and Alter assignments builds toward the ethical climax.

<Section icon={Target} title="Walkthrough: Reaching the Final Variable">

Navigating the final stages requires precise resource management. You cannot trigger **The Alters Last Variable secret endings** without successfully terraforming the high-tier arenas and powering the ENC Generator. The following steps outline the critical path from Cycle 4 to the credits.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Advanced Terraforming">
    Install Tier III Terraformers in the **Waterfall** and **Far Hill** regions. This requires a steady supply of Megafluid (90 units per Terraformer) and Phased Rapidium. These regions provide the final data points needed for Core Analysis.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The ENC Generator Project">
    Research the ENC Generator Alternator in the Research Lab. This project takes 12 working hours and requires 100 Proxylium and 100 Megafluid. Once researched, craft it in the Workshop and transport it to Rocky Valley.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Field Lab Assignments">
    Before the final descent, ensure your third Field Lab in Far Hill is fully operational. Assigning an Alter here is permanent; they will age as the planet's hot phase passes, providing a haunting visual reminder of the time Jan spent in cryosleep.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="The Descent">
    Once the generator is active, return to the base and inspect the laboratory board. Construct the final Elevator Section. This is your "Point of No Return." Ensure all personal conversations with your Alters are finished.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Strategy Success">
By creating a manual save before Step 4, you can easily reload to experience both endings and mop up any missing specialist achievements like **"Wait, you can do that?"** (creating the same Alter twice).
</Callout>

</Section>
