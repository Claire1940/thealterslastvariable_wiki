<Section icon={Shield} title="Cryosleep and the Final Variable">

The most significant mechanical difference in this story is Jan's ability to enter cryosleep. While this preserves your lifespan across solar cycles, your Alters remain awake. This creates a fascinating dynamic where the people you work with age and evolve while you remain static. This mechanic is the "Last Variable" that often dictates which ending you receive.
