<Section icon={Sparkles} title="The Decryption Process: Step-by-Step">

Once you have gathered the raw data or debris, you must follow a rigid scientific process to reveal the **The Alters Last Variable secret encrypted messages**. This process consumes both time and resources, making it essential to plan your decryption shifts during the "cool phase" of the planetary cycle.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Repair the Research Lab">
    Use 10 Metals to craft a Repair Kit in the Workshop. Apply this to the damaged Research Lab in the underground base to unlock the whiteboard and basic research functions.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Construct the Sample Analysis Module">
    Gather 60 Metals, 50 Rapidium, and 60 Organics. This module is the primary hardware used to "read" the encrypted data found in the Oasis and other arenas.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Initiate Magnetometric Analysis">
    For complex messages found in Misty Gorge, you must upgrade the module with a Magnetometer (30 Metals, 30 Minerals). This allows Jan to bypass the encryption on the Old Scientist's private logs.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Assign a Specialist">
    Place Jan Physicist or Jan Chemist in the lab. A Physicist can reduce the analysis time of technical debris, while a Chemist is better suited for biological data decryption.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Review the Decrypted Log">
    Access the laboratory board to read the final message. These logs often provide the "missing variables" needed to progress the main questline, such as the "Forced Dormancy" requirements.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Efficiency Bonus">
Completing the **Magnetometric Analysis** research project reduces all subsequent data decryption times by 15%, allowing you to clear the backlog of secret messages much faster in later cycles.
</Callout>

</Section>
