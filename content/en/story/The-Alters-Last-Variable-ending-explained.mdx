<Section icon={Target} title="How to Unlock Both Endings">

Unlocking both final paths requires completing the primary planetary research tree and assembling the final power infrastructure. Players do not need to replay the entire 20-hour campaign to view both outcomes if they utilize strategic saving.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Complete the Core Analysis">
    Research the **Core Analysis** project at the main laboratory desk, which requires 12 working hours of dedicated scientific labor.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Craft the Alternator">
    Gather the necessary materials to craft the **ENC Generator Alternator** inside your underground workshop, then transport it to Rocky Valley.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Create a Manual Save">
    Before interacting with the generator console to trigger the final sequence, create a manual backup save to easily reload later.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Make the Final Choice">
    Descend through the newly constructed Elevator Section, speak with JanBot, and select your preferred philosophical path.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Save Optimization Tip">
The game creates a reliable autosave immediately before the final JanBot conversation. You can reload this autosave directly from the main menu to unlock the second ending trophy without replaying the final generator defense.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Ending Breakdown: Detailed Paths & Consequences">

The two narrative branches represent fundamentally opposing views on Jan's scientific mission. The choice determines the legacy of the remaining specialist Alters and the ultimate meaning of the Oasis.

<CardGrid cols={2}>
  <Card title="Ending 1: Create a Successor">
    Jan decides that the secrets of the Oasis are too valuable to abandon. He authorizes JanBot to initiate a new cloning cycle, creating a fresh Jan replica to inherit the research.
    - **Pros**: The scientific mission continues indefinitely.
    - **Cons**: Condemns future clones to the same cycle of isolation.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Ending 2: Break the Cycle">
    Jan recognizes the ethical toll of creating artificial lives to serve his scientific obsession. He orders JanBot to disable the cloning systems and prepare the base for permanent shutdown.
    - **Pros**: Ends the exploitation of cloned personalities.
    - **Cons**: Leaves the ultimate mysteries of the planet unanswered.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

| Narrative Element | Create a Successor Path | Break the Cycle Path |
|---|---|---|
| **JanBot's Role** | Becomes the guardian of the next generation | Shuts down alongside base systems |
| **Alters' Legacy** | Their data is archived for future iterations | They live out their remaining natural days |
| **Oasis Status** | Subjected to continuous, aggressive analysis | Left undisturbed as a natural anomaly |

<Callout type="info" title="The Role of Field Laboratories">
Alters assigned to Field Laboratories in Rocky Valley, Misty Gorge, and Far Hill do not enter cryosleep with Jan. They age rapidly over the 13-year hot phases, serving as living monuments to Jan's single-minded focus on the planet's secrets.
</Callout>
