<Section icon={Map} title="Beginner Guide and First Cycle Walkthrough">

The opening of the expansion is a race against time. The planet undergoes extreme temperature shifts, and you must prepare your base for a multi-year "hot phase" where the surface becomes uninhabitable. The **The Alters Last Variable update 2.0.1 patch** balances the early resource scarcity, but careful management is still required.

Your primary objective in the first 14 days is the construction of the **Cryosleep Chamber**. Failure to meet this deadline results in the death of Jan and his Alters. Follow the build order below to ensure survival.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initial Repairs">
    Speak with the Old Scientist and complete his final request. Once he is buried, use your starting Metals to craft a **Repair Kit** at the Workshop and fix the damaged **Research Lab**.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Resource Outposts">
    Travel south to locate the Metals deposit. Build a **Metals Outpost** and connect it to your base. You will need a steady flow of Metals for every subsequent module.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="New Branching Procedure">
    Collect Rapidium from the nearby deposit. Research the **New Branching Procedure** in the lab. This allows you to create your first specialist Alter.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Oasis Investigation">
    Enter the Oasis and collect three **Electric Algae** samples. Return to the lab to start the **Analysis of Samples**, which unlocks the Sample Analysis Module.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Final Preparation">
    Construct the **Cryosleep Chamber** using 50 Metals and 65 Rapidium. Ensure you have at least 50 Organics per person before the 14-day timer expires.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Resource Bottlenecks">
Do not spend your Metals on "Comfort" modules like the Kitchen or specialized Dormitories until the Cryosleep Chamber and Metals Outpost are fully operational.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Trophy} title="Endings and Achievement Guide">

The conclusion of the expansion is determined by your ethical stance on the Oasis. After completing the **ENC Generator**, you will face a final conversation with JanBot. The choices you make here define the legacy of Jan Scientist and the fate of the Alters created during the campaign.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Ending: Create a Successor">
    Jan chooses to continue the research indefinitely. A new Alter and JanBot are tasked with carrying on the project, placing scientific discovery above the natural life cycle.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Ending: Break the Cycle">
    Jan rejects the endless creation of lives for the sake of a mystery. He accepts the limitations of his knowledge and allows the project to end, focusing on responsibility and restraint.
  </Step>
</Steps>

**Missable Achievements in Update 2.0.1:**
- **Wait, you can do that?**: Create the same specialist type for a second time (requires the first one to be assigned to a Field Lab).
- **Caring after all**: Complete all four Scientist-Alter storylines before the final generator sequence.
- **Space Gardener**: Successfully place and activate your first Terraformer in Rocky Valley.

<Callout type="warning" title="Spoiler Warning">
The final choice is permanent. Create a manual save before entering the Rocky Valley generator site if you wish to view both endings without replaying the entire 20-hour campaign.
</Callout>
