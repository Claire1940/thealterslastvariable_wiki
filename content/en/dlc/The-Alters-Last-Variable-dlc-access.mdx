<Section icon={Shield} title="Underground Base Prep and Cryosleep Loop">

Once you gain **The Alters Last Variable dlc access**, you are introduced to the core loop of the expansion. Unlike the mobile base in the main game, you manage a buried, underground research facility. The game structure revolves around cool exploration phases followed by intense, 13-year hot phases that force your crew into cryosleep.

Your immediate priority in Cycle 1 is building the Cryosleep Chamber within a strict 14-day deadline. If you fail to build this chamber or lack the necessary Organics to sustain your crew, you will face a game over.

| Base Action | Timing | Resource Cost | Primary Purpose | Failure Consequence |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Repair Research Lab** | Cycle 1, Day 1 | 10 Metals (Repair Kit) | Unlocks the Quantum Computer and starting research | Blocks all Alter creation and story progression |
| **Build Cryosleep Chamber** | Cycle 1, Days 1-14 | 50 Metals, 65 Rapidium | Protects the crew during the planet's hot phase | Hard game over when the cycle timer expires |
| **Reserve Cryosleep Organics** | Cycle End (Pre-Sleep) | 50 Organics per active Jan | Sustains the crew during long-term hibernation | Inability to enter cryosleep safely |
| **Dismantle Surface Outposts** | Cycle End (Pre-Sleep) | None | Recovers valuable resources before the hot phase | Surface structures are destroyed and resources lost |

<Callout type="success" title="Organics Management Tip">
Always treat your Organics stockpile as untouchable during the final days of a cycle. Spending your reserve on food or optional upgrades might leave you short of the 50 Organics per person required for cryosleep.
</Callout>
