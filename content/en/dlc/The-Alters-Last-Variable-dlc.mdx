<Section icon={Target} title="Mastering the Cryosleep and Aging Mechanics">

One of the most significant additions to the gameplay loop is the Cryosleep system. This mechanic allows Jan to skip through solar cycles to preserve his physical age, but it comes with a catch: the alters do not enter cryosleep with him. They remain awake, continuing their tasks, aging, and evolving their perspectives on the mission while Jan sleeps.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Task Assignment">
    Ensure all alters have long-term tasks assigned (mining, refining, or research) before entering the cryo-chamber.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Duration Selection">
    Choose the number of solar cycles to skip. Longer skips advance research faster but increase the risk of alter burnout.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Post-Sleep Debrief">
    Upon waking, immediately check the mood and age of your alters. Some may have shifted their demeanor or decided to slack off.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Conflict Resolution">
    Address any new conflicts that arose during your sleep. An alter who was reliable before may now be questioning the mission.
  </Step>
</Steps>

This creates a "randomness" factor where your base state can change significantly while you are inactive. It forces a balance between wanting to speed up progression and needing to keep a close eye on the social fabric of the bunker.

<Callout type="info" title="Cryosleep Strategy">
Use Cryosleep in short bursts initially. Long-term skips can result in alters reaching "retirement age" or developing negative traits that are difficult to reverse.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Swords} title="Resource Gathering and Exploration Hazards">

Venturing onto the surface remains a core part of the **The Alters Last Variable dlc** experience. The planet is still hostile, and the window for exploration is limited by the rising sun. New anomalies require the use of the Illuminator tool to disrupt time dilation and other environmental hazards.

| Hazard Type | Effect on Jan | Required Tool/Action |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Time Dilation** | Slows movement and resource gain | Illuminator disruption |
| **Radiation Pockets** | Rapid health depletion | Suit battery upgrades |
| **Structural Blockage** | Prevents access to shortcuts | Grappling gun / Heavy tools |
| **Solar Flare** | Instant death if caught outside | Return to bunker immediately |

Trial and error often govern these expeditions. You may find yourself halfway to a resource node only to realize your suit batteries are insufficient for the grappling gun required to cross a chasm. Efficient inventory management is key, as the storage in the bunker fills up quickly, necessitating frequent return trips.

<Callout type="success" title="Efficiency Tip">
Prioritize researching "Mining Speed" and "Storage Capacity" in the first few cycles. These two upgrades significantly reduce the number of trips required to maintain the bunker's energy needs.
</Callout>

</Section>
