<Section icon={Map} title="Step-by-Step Cycle 1 Survival Route">

The opening cycle of the expansion is the most challenging for new players. You must establish your economy and build the Cryosleep Chamber before the 14-day deadline expires.

Follow these steps to secure your base during Cycle 1:

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Bury the Old Scientist">
    Complete the opening quest *Famous Last Words* by speaking with the Old Scientist, burying him, and returning to JanBot. Inspect the damaged Research Lab and locked Quantum Computer.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Establish Metal Mining">
    Travel south from your base to locate the nearby Metals deposit. Build a Metals Outpost and connect it to your base network. Do not spend starting Metals on optional equipment yet.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Repair the Research Lab">
    Craft a Repair Kit in the Workshop for **10 Metals** and repair the damaged Research Lab. Start the *New Branching Procedure* research (takes 2 working hours and 20 Rapidium).
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Create your First Alter">
    Gather Rapidium from the deposit south of the Metals Outpost. Use the Quantum Computer to create **Jan Geologist** to instantly boost your Metal mining speed by 50%.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Investigate the Oasis">
    Enter the Oasis and collect all **3 Electric Algae samples**. Return to the base and begin the *Analysis of Samples* project in the Research Lab (takes 4 working hours).
  </Step>
  <Step num="6" title="Build the Cryosleep Chamber">
    Construct the Cryosleep Chamber using **50 Metals and 65 Rapidium**. Ensure you place it in a valid underground cell that does not block future corridor expansions.
  </Step>
</Steps>
