<Section icon={Shield} title="Underground Base Layout & Cryosleep Survival">

The underground base serves as your primary shield against the planet's extreme climate. The gameplay loop is defined by a 14-day deadline to construct the Cryosleep Chamber. Failing to build this module or failing to secure enough Organics before the hot phase begins will result in an immediate game over.

| Base System | Construction Cost | Primary Function | Common Failure Point |
|:---|:---|:---|:---|
| **Research Lab** | 10 Metals (Repair Kit) | Unlocks core technologies | Leaving it damaged blocks early Alter creation |
| **Cryosleep Chamber** | 50 Metals, 65 Rapidium | Survives the years-long hot phase | Delays can leave too few days to build it |
| **Metals Outpost** | 10 Metals (Pylons) | Automates resource extraction | Relying only on surface piles wastes work hours |
| **Sample Analysis** | 60 Metals, 50 Rapidium, 60 Organics | Analyzes Oasis discoveries | Building it before securing cryosleep reserves |

<Callout type="warning" title="Cryosleep Resource Lock">
You must reserve 50 Organics for every person entering cryosleep. For Jan Scientist and two Alters, you must keep 150 Organics untouched in your storage. Do not accidentally spend this reserve on food or crafting on the final day.
</Callout>

When laying out your base, pay close attention to soil restrictions. Avoid placing the massive Cryosleep Chamber in a location that cuts off future corridors. Keep your storage rooms close to the Workshop to minimize walking times for your busy Alters.

</Section>
