<Section icon={Sparkles} title="The Variable of Time: Cryosleep and Aging">

One of the most significant additions in Last Variable is the **Cryosleep** mechanic. This allows the Jan Scientist to preserve his lifespan across solar cycles, but it comes with a psychological cost: your Alters do not sleep. They remain awake, continuing their tasks, which causes them to age and evolve independently of you.

This leads to fascinating narrative shifts. A reliable Alter might become cynical after decades of waiting for the Scientist to wake up, or they might develop entirely new hobbies and perspectives. This "randomness" keeps the late-game experience fresh, as you never know what version of your "self" you will wake up to.

<Callout type="tip" title="Cryosleep Strategy">
Only enter Cryosleep when your current research goals are met. Leaving Alters unattended for too many cycles can lead to permanent changes in their demeanor and productivity.
</Callout>

| Mechanic | Effect on Jan Scientist | Effect on Alters | Impact on Gameplay |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Normal Play** | Active resource gathering | Direct task management | Standard survival loop |
| **Cryosleep** | Preservation of life | Physical aging/Maturity | Narrative evolution |
| **Time Dilation** | Disrupts movement | No effect | Environmental hazard |

</Section>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Technical Performance and Presentation">

Visually, Last Variable maintains the high standard set by 11-bit studios. The "Oasis" is a lush, vibrant contrast to the barren wasteland of the base game. The character models are highly detailed, with facial expressions that convey the nuances of each Jan's personality.

However, the technical side isn't without minor flaws. Particle-heavy scenes, particularly when using the Illuminator during an anomaly, can cause noticeable frame drops even on high-end hardware. There are also occasional pathfinding bugs where Alters might face the wrong direction during pivotal dialogue scenes.

| :--- | :--- | :--- |

| **Controller Support** | Excellent | Native support for all major gamepads |
