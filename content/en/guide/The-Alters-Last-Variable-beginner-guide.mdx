<Section icon={Target} title="The Alters Last Variable Beginner Guide: Cycle 1 Route">

Navigating the opening cycle of **The Alters Last Variable beginner guide** requires strict adherence to a specific build order. Your primary goal is to establish automated resource collection, repair your core research facilities, and build the Cryosleep Chamber before the 14-day deadline expires.

### Opening Build Order Comparison

| Phase | Core Objective | Key Resource Cost | Primary Hazard |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Days 1–2** | Repair Research Lab & Build Metals Outpost | 10 Metals (Repair Kit) | Resource starvation |
| **Days 3–4** | Create First Alter & Probe Oasis | 50 Metals, 65 Rapidium | Time constraints |
| **Days 5–6** | Construct Sample Analysis Module | 60 Metals, 50 Rapidium, 60 Organics | Power overload |

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Complete Famous Last Words">
    Speak with the Old Scientist to complete his final request. Bury him, return to JanBot, and inspect the damaged Research Lab and locked Quantum Computer. Save your starting Metals; do not craft optional gear yet.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Establish the Metals Outpost">
    Head south of the base to locate the nearest Metals deposit. Construct a Metals Outpost and connect it to your base network. Build a Repair Kit in the Workshop using 10 Metals and repair the Research Lab.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Research New Branching Procedure">
    Extract Rapidium from the deposit south of the Metals Outpost. Use your newly repaired Research Lab to research the New Branching Procedure. This project costs 20 Rapidium and takes 2 working hours.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Create Your First Alter">
    Access the Quantum Computer to create your first specialist Alter. For the optimal opening route, select Jan Geologist to resolve early-game extraction bottlenecks.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Investigate the Oasis">
    Travel to the Oasis and gather all three Electric Algae samples. Do not leave early; collecting all three in a single run saves valuable exploration hours. Return to the lab and begin the Analysis of Samples.
  </Step>
  <Steps num="6" title="Build the Cryosleep Chamber">
    Construct the Cryosleep Chamber using 50 Metals and 65 Rapidium. Ensure you place it in a cell that does not block future corridor expansions through the underground soil.
  </Steps>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Deadline Alert">
Delaying the construction of the Cryosleep Chamber beyond Day 14 will result in an immediate campaign failure when the hot phase hits.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Underground Base and Cryosleep Maintenance">

The underground base requires careful spatial planning. Soil variations restrict where you can place specific modules, making efficient layouts crucial for minimizing walking distances and power consumption.

### Critical Cryosleep Organics Requirements

To survive the transition between planetary cycles, you must stockpile a strict amount of Organics before entering cryosleep. 

| Crew Size | Required Organics | Survival Outcome |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Jan Scientist Only** | 50 Organics | Safe transition to next cycle |
| **Jan + 1 Alter** | 100 Organics | Safe transition to next cycle |
| **Jan + 2 Alters** | 150 Organics | Safe transition to next cycle |
| **Any Crew Size** | Less than required | Critical starvation / Crew loss |

<Callout type="success" title="Dismantling Tip">
On the final evening of a cycle, dismantle your surface structures (Outposts, Phasers, Pipelines). This returns their construction materials directly to your base inventory, preventing decay.
</Callout>
