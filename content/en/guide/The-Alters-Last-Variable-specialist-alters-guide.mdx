In the 2026 expansion *The Alters: Last Variable*, the survival of Jan Scientist depends entirely on his ability to manifest specific scientific versions of himself. Each specialist brings a unique production bonus, a specific research acceleration capability, and a distinct personality that influences your underground base management.

<Section icon={Swords} title="Detailed Specialist Breakdown and Bonuses">

Each specialist in *The Alters: Last Variable* provides a passive boost to specific base operations. To maximize your efficiency, you must assign the correct Jan to the corresponding module. This **The Alters Last Variable specialist alters guide** recommends keeping a close eye on the "Scientific Specialty" of each crew member.

### Jan Geologist: The Foundation
The Geologist is your primary explorer. He excels at identifying high-yield deposits and is the only specialist capable of researching the "Root-barrier Drilling" technology needed to access the Oasis routes in Cycle 2.

### Jan Physicist: The Engineer
The Physicist is essential for the transition into the mid-game. As your base expands, earthquake damage becomes more frequent. The Physicist’s 50% maintenance bonus ensures that your life support and power systems remain online with minimal Metal expenditure.

### Jan Chemist: The Researcher
The Chemist becomes the MVP during Cycle 3 and Cycle 4. When you unlock the Field Laboratories in Rocky Valley and Misty Gorge, his ability to cut research time by nearly 40% allows you to finish projects before the planetary radiation waves force you back into the base.

<Section icon={Target} title="Field Laboratory Assignment Guide">

A unique mechanic in the *Last Variable* expansion is the Field Laboratory. These outposts allow you to conduct advanced research on the planet's surface, but they require a permanent specialist assignment.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Unlock the Arena">
    Terraform the target region (e.g., Rocky Valley for the first lab) to reveal the construction site.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Prepare the Specialist">
    Complete all personal conversations and urgent base-side research for the Alter you plan to send.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Deploy the Lab">
    Construct the Field Lab using 50 Metals, 50 Minerals, and 100 Organics.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Commit the Assignment">
    Assign the specialist. Note: This Alter will no longer assist with base chores and will age visibly across cycles.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Permanent Choice">
Once an Alter is assigned to a Field Lab, they are locked out of base production. Do not send your only Geologist if you still have major mining operations that require his 50% bonus.
</Callout>

</Section>

### Managing Personalities and Relationships
- **Geologist**: Enthusiastic. Support his "controlled experiments" to gain temporary research boosts.
- **Physicist**: Status-conscious. Avoid dismissive dialogue; give him high-priority tasks to keep his morale high.
- **Chemist**: Pragmatic. Values efficiency. Do not assign him to low-value work like cleaning or simple hauling.
- **Biologist**: Idealistic. Complete his "plant-related requests" to unlock the "Morale Recovery" bonus for the whole crew.

<Callout type="tip" title="Maintenance Tip">
During the "Hot Phase," base damage is inevitable. Ensure your Physicist has a stockpile of at least 5 Repair Kits in the storage module before you enter cryosleep.
</Callout>

### The Final Variable: Successor vs. Break the Cycle
The expansion concludes with a decisive choice regarding the Oasis. Your relationship with your specialist Alters will color how these endings feel.

1. **Create a Successor**: Jan chooses to continue the research indefinitely by creating a new version of himself to carry the torch. This represents a commitment to scientific obsession over human mortality.
2. **Break the Cycle**: Jan accepts the limits of his life and the project. He chooses to end the creation of Alters, prioritizing ethical responsibility and closure.
