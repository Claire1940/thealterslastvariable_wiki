<Section icon={Map} title="The Alters Last Variable Underground Base Guide: Construction Basics">

Building and maintaining your facility is the core loop of [The Alters: Last Variable](https://store.steampowered.com/app/3237520/The_Alters_Last_Variable/). Unlike the mobile mobile base in the original game, the expansion tasks Jan Scientist with reclaiming a buried, static research station. Because you cannot move this base away from planetary hazards, your layout decisions, power grids, and expansion routes dictate your survival. 

Every room you build must fit within specific underground grids. Soil density, structural damage from earthquakes, and radiation waves limit where and when you can expand.

| Construction Element | Primary Resource | Primary Function | Placement Rule |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Corridors** | Metals | Connects rooms | Always build horizontal backbones first |
| **Power Pylons** | Metals, Rapidium | Expands grid range | Place near outer resource outposts |
| **Storage Modules** | Metals | Holds physical items | Keep adjacent to Workshops and Refineries |
| **Life Support** | Metals, Organics | Maintains crew health | Isolate from high-noise power generators |

<Callout type="tip" title="Soil Restriction Warning">
Not all underground tiles are buildable. Hard rock and radioactive soil deposits require specific drill upgrades or anomaly repulsors before you can clear them. Always preview room footprints before committing precious Metals.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Target} title="How to Start and Build Your Core Base Modules">

Starting your expansion run correctly prevents early resource bankruptcy. Follow this step-by-step path to establish your core infrastructure, unlock your first specialist Alter, and secure your survival before the first hot phase.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Repair the Research Core">
    Collect surface Metals immediately on Day 1. Build a Repair Kit in your Workshop for 10 Metals and apply it to the damaged Research Lab. This unlocks the whiteboard and allows you to analyze the new branching procedure.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Establish Automated Resource Extraction">
    Do not rely on manual surface gathering. Travel south of your base, locate the primary Metals deposit, and build your first Metals Outpost. Connect it back to the base grid using power pylons.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Construct the Cryosleep Chamber">
    The Forced Dormancy mission gives you a strict 14-day deadline. Reserve 50 Metals and 65 Rapidium to build the Cryosleep Chamber. Place it in a central, safe soil tile where it won't block future corridor expansions.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Deploy the Sample Analysis Module">
    Once you venture into the Oasis and collect three biological samples, research Preliminary Analysis. Build the Sample Analysis Module using 60 Metals, 50 Rapidium, and 60 Organics to begin advanced tech trees.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Ideal First Alter Creation">
Create **Jan Geologist** as your first Alter. His 50% mining efficiency bonus for Metals and rapid progression through geology tech trees will ease the resource strain of early base construction.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Shield} title="The Cryosleep Cycle: Hot Phase Preparation Checklist">

The planet's climate shifts between cool exploration phases and scorching, years-long hot phases. Before you put Jan and his Alters into cryosleep, you must run a strict logistics checklist to ensure you do not wake up to a broken base and starved crew.

| Resource Type | Minimum Cryosleep Reserve | Failure Consequence |
|---|---|---|
| **Organics** | 50 per person in base | Crew starvation / Health decay |
| **Metals** | 30 in storage | Cannot repair earthquake damage on day 1 |
| **Repair Kits** | 2 Kits in Workshop | Critical systems remain offline after waking |
| **Power Cells** | 3 fully charged | Life support fails during the wake-up shift |
