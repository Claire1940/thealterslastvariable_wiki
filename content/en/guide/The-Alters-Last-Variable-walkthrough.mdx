<Section icon={Target} title="The Alters Last Variable Walkthrough: Cycle-by-Cycle Guide">

Surviving the harsh planetary shifts requires strict adherence to daily schedules. This step-by-step **The Alters Last Variable walkthrough** guides you through the critical objectives of each planetary cycle.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Cycle 1: The First Branch">
    Your journey begins with the quest **Famous Last Words**. Locate and speak with the Old Scientist to fulfill his final request, then bury him. Return to JanBot and inspect the damaged Research Lab. You must research the **New Branching Procedure** (costs 20 Rapidium, takes 2 working hours) and build your first Metals Outpost to the south. You have a strict 14-day deadline to build the **Cryosleep Chamber** (costs 50 Metals, 65 Rapidium) under the quest **Forced Dormancy**. Collect three Oasis samples to construct the **Sample Analysis Module** (costs 60 Metals, 50 Rapidium, 60 Organics) and install your first Terraformer in Rocky Valley before entering cryosleep.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Cycle 2: Oasis 2.0">
    Upon waking, restore the resource outposts dismantled during the transition. Under the main quest **Oasis 2.0**, research **Drilling Through Root Barriers** to bypass planetary blockades. Explore the newly opened routes within the Oasis. Your primary structural goal is to build the first **Field Laboratory** (costs 50 Metals, 50 Minerals, 100 Organics) on the second tier near the Metals Outpost. Assign a specialist Alter to this outpost before the final day of the cycle.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Cycle 3: Proxilium and Misty Gorge">
    Speak with JanBot at the Field Laboratory to unlock **Proxilium** processing. Build a **Proxilium Tank** (costs 150 Metals, 40 Minerals) and construct the **Splitter** (costs 300 Metals, 150 Minerals, 100 Organics) to produce advanced materials. Research and deploy a Tier II Terraformer in Misty Gorge. Finally, upgrade your base with a **Magnetometer** (costs 30 Metals, 30 Minerals) to complete the 12-hour **Magnetometric Analysis**.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Cycle 4: Quantum Biofield">
    Investigate the planet's rising radiation waves. Complete the **Radiation Crystallization** experiment (takes 12 working hours) to unlock pathways leading to the elevated central area of Misty Gorge. Construct the second Field Laboratory here and assign an Alter to secure the research data before the cycle expires.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Advanced Cycles: Megafluid Tech">
    Combine data from both active Field Laboratories to unlock **Megafluid** production. You must construct and activate two Tier III Terraformers (costs 90 Metals, 200 Rapidium, 75 Megafluid each) in Far Hill and Waterfall. Upgrade the Sample Analysis Module with a **Mass Spectrometer** (costs 50 Metals, 50 Minerals) to perform the 12-hour **Mass Spectrometric Analysis**, then deploy the third Field Laboratory in Far Hill.
  </Step>
  <Step num="6" title="Final Cycle: Energy Extraction">
    Analyze the planetary core (takes 12 working hours). Build the **Resistor Blocks** (costs 50 Metals, 300 Phased Rapidium, 200 Proxilium, 100 Megafluid) and craft the **ENC Generator Alternator** (costs 50 Metals, 50 Organics, 160 Special Resources). Assemble the generator, place it in Rocky Valley, and activate it. Descend through the newly constructed base Elevator Section to reach the final chamber.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Resource Warning">
Do not leave the Oasis during Cycle 1 after collecting only one or two samples. A second trip consumes an entire block of working hours, which can ruin your early build order.
</Callout>

### The Cryosleep Checklist

Before ending any cycle, you must complete the following steps to avoid critical failures or clone deaths upon waking:

1. **Secure Organics**: Stockpile exactly 50 Organics for each clone remaining in the base (e.g., 150 Organics for Jan plus two base-bound Alters).
2. **Dismantle Surface Outposts**: Deconstruct temporary pipelines and outposts to recoup materials before the planetary shift destroys them.
3. **Heal the Crew**: Ensure all clones are fully healed in the Infirmary and have low radiation exposure levels.
4. **Assign Field Lab Workers**: Place your chosen researcher in the active Field Lab; they do not consume base Organics while stationed outside.

<Callout type="warning" title="Critical Warning">
Failing to meet the Organics requirement before entering cryosleep will result in the permanent death of your unsupplied Alters, severely crippling your research capacity in the next cycle.
</Callout>
