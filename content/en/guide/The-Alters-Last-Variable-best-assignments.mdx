### Managing Field Laboratory Deployments

Field Laboratories are located outside the base and are vital for advancing your understanding of the planet. However, sending a specialist to a Field Lab is a double-edged sword.

- **The Trade-Off**: Stationing an Alter in a Field Lab removes them from your active base roster. They will stop contributing to daily chores, crafting, and resource gathering.
- **The Aging Mechanic**: Assigned Alters stay on the surface while Jan Scientist enters cryosleep. They will visibly age across the planetary cycles.
- **The Solution**: Complete all of an Alter's urgent base upgrades, personal conversations, and story objectives before permanently deploying them to a Field Lab.

<Callout type="warning" title="Crucial Warning: Stationing Limits">
Once an Alter is assigned to a Field Lab, they are locked into that role for the remainder of the cycle. Ensure your base has a comfortable buffer of crafted items (like Repair Kits) before sending Jan Physicist or Jan Geologist to the surface.
</Callout>

  <Step num="1" title="Bury the Old Scientist">

  <Step num="2" title="Establish Metal Income">

  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Repair and Research">
    Use the Repair Kit to restore the Research Lab. Interact with the laboratory whiteboard and begin researching the **New Branching Procedure** (requires 20 Rapidium and 2 working hours) to unlock the cloning system.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Clone Jan Geologist">
    Gather Rapidium from the deposit south of your Metals Outpost. Use the Quantum Computer to clone **Jan Geologist** as your first specialist. Immediately assign him to mine the southern Metal deposits to double your income.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Investigate the Oasis">

  <Step num="6" title="Build the Core Modules">

  </Step>
</Steps>

### Soil and Layout Optimization

When expanding your underground base, you must plan around varied soil types and module footprints. Avoid placing critical rooms in isolated corners.

| Room Type | Placement Rule | Failure Consequence |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Cryosleep Chamber** | Place early in a central cell. | Blocks future corridor expansions. |
| **Research Lab & Workshop** | Keep adjacent to storage modules. | Increases walking times and wastes work hours. |
| **Dormitory & Mess Hall** | Separate from noisy machinery. | Lowers crew morale and triggers relationship conflicts. |
| **Power & Life Support** | Build near the elevator shaft. | Increases pylon costs and complicates repairs. |

<Callout type="info" title="Morale Management">
Alters who work long shifts without rest or sleep in noisy dormitories will quickly build up stress. Use Jan Biologist in the Greenhouse to grow comfort crops, or assign stressed crew members to Recreation shifts to keep their productivity high.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Trophy} title="Endings, Missables & Achievements">

The final segment of the expansion brings Jan Scientist face-to-face with the ultimate consequences of his obsession. While most dialogue choices shape personal relationships, the definitive ending branch is decided during your final conversation with JanBot after activating the ENC Generator in Rocky Valley.

### The Two Concluding Paths

- **Ending 1: Create a Successor**: Jan accepts a self-perpetuating cycle of research. A new clone and JanBot are designated to continue studying the Oasis, prioritizing scientific discovery over individual human lifetimes.
- **Ending 2: Break the Cycle**: Jan rejects the cloning model. He accepts his own mortality and decides that some questions are better left unanswered, sparing future Jans from carrying the same burden.

### Missable Content & Save Points

To experience everything the expansion has to offer in a single playthrough, utilize these strategic save points:

1. **Before Field Lab Assignments**: Save before locking a specialist into a Field Lab. This allows you to finish their personal storylines first.
2. **Before the ENC Generator**: Create a manual backup save before assembling the generator. This is your last chance to complete surface exploration and clean up missing achievements.
3. **The Final Conversation**: The game creates an autosave right before you speak to JanBot. Use this to easily view both endings without replaying the campaign.
