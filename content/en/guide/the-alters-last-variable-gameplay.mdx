Surviving these deadly phases requires building a functional underground base and utilizing advanced survival machinery.

  <Step num="3" title="Manage Crew Fatigue">

It buys time, allowing you to witness the long-term effects of your scientific endeavors without exposing your team to lethal radiation. However, cryo-slumber is not a perfect shield; the stress of prolonged sleep gradually reduces your crew's efficiency upon waking.

<Callout type="tip" title="Cryosleep Preparation">
Ensure all reactors are fully fueled and base defenses are active before initiating cryosleep. A power failure during a cycle change can lead to catastrophic module damage and clone death.
