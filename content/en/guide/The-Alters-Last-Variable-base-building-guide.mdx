<Section icon={Map} title="Core Mechanics of Underground Construction">

Building a functional underground base in *The Alters: Last Variable* requires a deep understanding of structural layouts, soil restrictions, and module connectivity. Unlike the mobile base of the main game, your underground facility is fixed in place, meaning poor initial planning can permanently block essential expansion pathways. 

The underground grid consists of distinct soil types that dictate where specific modules can be placed. Building over unstable soil without proper reinforcement will lead to frequent structural damage, especially during the planet's recurring earthquakes.

| Soil Type | Structural Stability | Best Module Matches | Construction Restrictions |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Stable Rock** | Excellent | Heavy machinery, Workshop, Refinery | None; ideal for high-load rooms |
| **Loose Soil** | Low | Dormitories, Infirmary, Greenhouse | Requires reinforcement; high damage risk |
| **Fissured Soil** | Critical | Power lines, simple corridors | Do not place production modules here |

<Callout type="warning" title="Soil Warning">
Never place the Research Lab or the Workshop in Fissured Soil cells. An earthquake will instantly disable these facilities, halting your active research projects and item crafting queues.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Shield} title="The Alters Last Variable Base Building Guide: Step-by-Step Layout">

To successfully establish your underground base and survive the strict 14-day Cryosleep Chamber deadline in Cycle 1, you must follow a highly optimized build order. Wasting starting Metals on non-essential structures will leave you vulnerable to early resource bottlenecks.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Restore the Research Core">
    Immediately construct a Repair Kit in the Workshop using **10 Metals**. Use it to repair the damaged Research Lab. Once online, interact with the laboratory whiteboard and begin researching the **New Branching Procedure** to unlock the specialist crew path.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Establish Resource Outposts">
    Travel south from your base to locate the nearest Metals deposit. Build a Metals Outpost and connect it to your base network. This automated supply is crucial for sustaining your daily construction requirements.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Build the Cryosleep Chamber">
    Before Day 14, you must construct the Cryosleep Chamber to survive the upcoming hot phase. This requires **50 Metals and 65 Rapidium**. Ensure you place this module in a central, easily accessible cell connected to your main power lines.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Construct the Sample Analysis Module">
    After collecting the three essential samples from the Oasis, invest **60 Metals, 50 Rapidium, and 60 Organics** to construct the Sample Analysis Module. This unlocks the advanced research tree required for higher-tier terraforming.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Build Order Tip">
Always maintain a reserve of **20 Metals** in your inventory. This ensures you can instantly craft Drill Cartridges or Repair Kits if an earthquake blocks your resource pipelines or damages your life support.
</Callout>

</Section>
