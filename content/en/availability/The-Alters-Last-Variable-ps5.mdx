The final moments of *The Alters Last Variable* test your scientific ethics. All major choices lead to one of two distinct philosophical conclusions.

### The Decisive Final Choice
After assembling and activating the ENC Generator in Rocky Valley, return to the base and descend into the deep elevator section to speak with JanBot:

- **Ending 1: Create a Successor**: Jan chooses to clone a new researcher to carry on the work. JanBot and the new Alter continue the research indefinitely. This path values continuity and the pursuit of knowledge above all else.
