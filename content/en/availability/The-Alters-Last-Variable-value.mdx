The narrative of this expansion picks up from one of the base game's most intriguing endings. After the original Jan Dolski finds himself stranded, he creates a specialized Jan scientist to continue the mission of researching the "Oasis"—a mysterious site capable of surviving the planet's devastating sunrise. Understanding **The Alters Last Variable value** requires a shift in perspective; you are no longer managing a massive mobile base, but rather a claustrophobic, high-stakes bunker environment where every decision impacts the survival of your scientific team.

**Video Highlights:**
- **Standalone Story**: Picks up from the ending where Jan remains on the planet.
- **Bunker Management**: Shifting from a mobile base to a stationary underground laboratory.
- **Character Evolution**: Alters now age and change their demeanor based on your interactions and time spent in cryosleep.
- **Research Focus**: The main goal is advancing scientific theories on the whiteboard to unlock the secrets of the Oasis.

<Section icon={Zap} title="Survival and Resource Management">

Resource management is the backbone of the survival loop. In the bunker, space is at a premium, making the base-building aspect more of a puzzle than in the original game. You must carefully manage your Rapidium levels, as this substance is essential for creating new Alters and powering the bunker's shielding.

**Key Resource Types:**
- **Rapidium**: Used for Alter creation and advanced technology.
- **ALX**: The primary material for construction and maintenance.
- **Battery Power**: Essential for surface exploration and tool usage.
- **Radiation Shielding**: Consumable items that protect the base during solar flares.

| Resource | Primary Source | Usage | Scarcity Level |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Rapidium** | Deep Veins | Alter Creation, Fuel | High |
| **ALX** | Surface Rocks | Construction, Repairs | Medium |
| **Organics** | Oasis Flora | Food, Medicine | Low |
| **Silicates** | Sandy Nodes | Glass, Electronics | Medium |

The "Trial and Error" nature of expeditions means you will often fail before you succeed. Learning the shortcuts through the Oasis and identifying which anomalies require the Illuminator to disrupt is vital for maximizing your limited window on the surface.

<Callout type="success" title="Efficiency Secret">
Use the "Swap Task" keybind to instantly move Alters between the Workshop and the Research Lab. This minimizes downtime and keeps your progression steady.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Clock} title="The Cryosleep and Aging System">

The most significant mechanical innovation in the DLC is the Cryosleep system. This allows the player character to preserve his lifespan across multiple solar cycles. However, there is a catch: your Alters do not enter cryosleep with you. They remain awake, continuing their assigned tasks, but they also age and evolve.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Assign Long-term Tasks">
    Before entering sleep, ensure all Alters have enough materials to continue research or mining.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Set Sleep Duration">
    Choose how many solar cycles you wish to skip based on your current research requirements.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Monitor Aging Effects">
    Check the status of your Alters upon waking. They may have changed their outlook or developed new conflicts.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Address Conflicts">
    Resolve any social issues that arose while you were asleep to restore base efficiency.
  </Step>
</Steps>

This mechanic adds a layer of unpredictability. A reliable Alter might become cynical after years of working alone while you slept, leading to new dialogue branches and potential mission failures. It forces you to weigh the benefits of fast-forwarding research against the risk of social instability within the bunker.

| Sleep Duration | Player Benefit | Alter Risk | Outcome |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Short (1 Cycle)** | Minor stamina recovery | Low stress | Minimal changes |
| **Medium (3 Cycles)** | Significant research progress | Moderate fatigue | New dialogue options |
| **Long (5+ Cycles)** | Breakthroughs unlocked | High risk of slacking | Character evolution/Aging |

<Callout type="info" title="Social Dynamics">
The writing ensures that you care about these versions of yourself. Aging isn't just a stat change; it's a narrative shift that alters how the Jans interact with each other.
</Callout>
