<CardGrid cols={2}>
  <Card title="Do: Establish Automated Mining">
    Immediately build a **Metals Outpost** south of your starting base. Manual scavenging on the surface is too slow to meet the 14-day construction deadline.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Do: Analyze Oasis Samples">
    Collect all **3 Electric Algae samples** from the Oasis on your first expedition. Run the analysis in the Research Lab to unlock advanced technology.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Don't: Build Comfort Modules Early">
    Avoid building advanced crew quarters or decorative base modules until your **Cryosleep Chamber** (50 Metals, 65 Rapidium) is fully constructed.

  <Card title="Don't: Overproduce Food">

<Section icon={Shield} title="Underground Base Layout & Cryosleep Checklist">

The underground base requires careful planning. Soil restrictions limit where you can place specific rooms, and earthquakes will regularly damage your modules. Before entering cryosleep at the end of a cool phase, you must complete a strict preparation checklist.

### Recommended Base Layout Phases

- **Early Game Layout**: Place the **Research Lab** and **Workshop** close to the central elevator. Build the **Dormitory** away from noisy machinery to preserve crew morale.
- **Mid-Game Layout**: Reserve a large, unobstructed soil patch for the **Sample Analysis Module** and the **Cryosleep Chamber**.
- **Late Game Layout**: Connect the **Splitter** and advanced processing rooms directly to your storage facilities to minimize transit times.

| Action Timing | Required Resources | Target Objective | Failure Consequence |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Cycle 1, Day 1** | 10 Metals | Repair the Research Lab | Blocks all technology and Alter creation |
| **Before Day 14** | 50 Metals, 65 Rapidium | Build Cryosleep Chamber | Instant game over due to surface heat |
| **End of Every Cycle** | 50 Organics per person | Stock Cryosleep Chamber | Crew members perish during sleep |
| **Final Evening** | 0 Resources | Dismantle surface outposts | Permanent loss of outpost construction parts |

<Callout type="warning" title="Morale Management">
Never send your crew to sleep while they are injured or suffering from extreme radiation. Low health and poor morale can trigger an **Alter Revolt** immediately upon waking in the next cycle.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Swords} title="Specialist Alters & Best Work Assignments">

The expansion introduces **4 specialist Alters**, each boasting unique production bonuses, research paths, and personalities. Managing their workloads and placing them in the correct roles is key to keeping the base running smoothly.

### Specialist Tier List & Role Assignments

- **Jan Geologist (S-Tier)**: The ultimate early-game Alter. He provides a **50% mining speed boost** and accelerates Phased Rapidium research. Keep him mining or exploring the surface.
- **Jan Physicist (A-Tier)**: The master of the Workshop. He increases **crafting speed by 50%** and reduces base maintenance costs. Assign him to craft Repair Kits and process advanced materials.
- **Jan Chemist (A-Tier)**: Essential for the **Field Laboratory** mechanics. He completes complex Field Lab research in 6 hours and 15 minutes (compared to 10 hours for other Alters).
- **Jan Biologist (A-Tier)**: The ultimate support unit. He works the **Greenhouse with 100% efficiency** and heals injured crew members 8 times faster in the Infirmary.

<Callout type="success" title="Field Lab Deployment Strategy">
Before deploying an Alter to an external Field Lab, ensure you have completed their personal story quests and base-level conversations. While stationed at a Field Lab, they cannot contribute to daily base operations.
</Callout>

</Section>

### Campaign Endings & Final Decisions

Your dialogue choices throughout the story build relationships, but the ultimate ending is decided by a final choice when interacting with **JanBot** after activating the ENC Generator:

1. **Ending 1: Create a Successor**: Jan Scientist decides to create a continuous line of clones to ensure the Oasis research survives forever. This prioritizes the pursuit of knowledge above individual lives.
2. **Ending 2: Break the Cycle**: Jan Scientist refuses to burden another clone with this endless survival loop, choosing to shut down the project and accept mortality.

<Callout type="tip" title="Easy Completion Tip">
