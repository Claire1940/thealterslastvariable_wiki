<Section icon={Target} title="How to Access and Start the Expansion">

Accessing the *Last Variable* content is straightforward, but it follows a specific narrative path. The expansion assumes the "Scientist Stayed" ending from the base game. However, you do not need a completed save file to begin; you can launch the DLC as a separate campaign directly from the main menu. This allows both returning veterans and new players to jump straight into the new 20-hour story.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Verify Installation">
    Ensure *The Alters* base game is updated to the latest version. The expansion content is integrated into the core game files but remains locked until the DLC license is detected.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Select New Game">
    Launch the game and navigate to the "Start New Game" menu. You will see two options: the original "The Alters" campaign and the "Last Variable" expansion campaign.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Confirm Story Path">
    Select "Last Variable." A brief cinematic will recap the events leading to Jan Scientist's isolation on the planet, setting the stage for the underground base construction.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Initialize Underground Base">
    The game begins with Jan Scientist and JanBot inspecting the damaged Research Lab. Follow the initial prompts to secure your first 10 Metals for the mandatory Repair Kit.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Save File Separation">
Progress in *Last Variable* is saved in a separate slot from the base game. Resources, base layouts, and Alter relationships from your original playthrough do not carry over, as this represents a localized story with entirely new specialist Alters.
</Callout>

</Section>

Choosing the order in which you create these specialists is the most critical decision in the first cycle.

<Section icon={Shield} title="Cycle 1: The 14-Day Survival Deadline">

The first cycle of *The Alters Last Variable* is a race against time. You have exactly 14 days to construct the **Cryosleep Chamber** before the planet's "hot phase" begins. Failure to meet this deadline results in an immediate game over. You must balance repairing the Research Lab, establishing a Metal outpost, and creating your first Alter while saving enough resources for the chamber.

| Requirement | Resource Cost | Time Cost | Importance |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Repair Kit** | 10 Metals | 1 Hour | Mandatory for Lab |
| **New Branching** | 20 Rapidium | 2 Hours | Unlocks Specialists |
| **Cryosleep Chamber**| 50 Metals / 65 Rapidium | 4 Hours | **Survival Deadline** |
| **Sample Module** | 60 Metals / 50 Rapidium | 3 Hours | Story Progression |

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initial Salvage">
    Immediately collect the 10 Metals located south of the base. Do not build optional modules like storage until the Research Lab is repaired.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The First Outpost">
    Build a Metals Outpost in Rocky Valley. Assign Jan Scientist to manual collection until you can create Jan Geologist to automate the process.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Rapid Research">
    Prioritize "Analysis of Samples" to unlock the Oasis route. This provides the Rapidium needed for both Alter creation and the Cryosleep Chamber.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Deadline Check">
    By Day 10, you should have the 50 Metals and 65 Rapidium reserved. If you are short, dismantle non-essential surface structures to reclaim 50% of their build cost.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Optimization Bonus">
Creating Jan Geologist on Day 3 allows you to reach the Metal requirement for the Cryosleep Chamber by Day 7, giving you a full week to focus on optional story objectives and relationship building.
</Callout>

</Section>
